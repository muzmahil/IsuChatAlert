# IsuChatAlert | Chat Alert App for Assetto Corsa

![Assetto Corsa](https://img.shields.io/badge/Assetto_Corsa-App-red.svg)
![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.3-blue.svg)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green.svg)

Never miss your name or important messages in crowded multiplayer servers! **IsuChatAlert** is a lightweight, highly customizable chat notification UI app for Assetto Corsa. 

## ✨ Features

* **Auto-Mention Alert:** Automatically detects your in-game driver name. If another player mentions you in the chat, a pop-up appears with a notification sound.
* **Custom Keyword Tracking:** Looking out for specific words like "pit", "pass", or "blue flag"? Enter your custom keywords and get alerted instantly.
* **Fully Customizable HUD:** Adjust text size, UI scale, background opacity, and choose from multiple accent colors to match your layout.
* **Smart Display Time:** The notification pop-up dynamically adjusts its display time based on the length of the message.
* **Audio Feedback:** Includes a subtle alert sound to grab your attention without being distracting (can be muted).
* **Draggable & Pinnable:** Move the alert box anywhere on your screen and pin it!

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the latest release or clone this repository.
2. Extract the contents and drag the `apps` folder into your main Assetto Corsa root directory:
   *(Usually located at `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\assettocorsa`)*
3. Open Content Manager (or the original Assetto Corsa launcher).
4. Navigate to **Settings > Assetto Corsa > Apps** and check the box next to **IsuChatAlert** to enable the app.

## 🚀 Usage & Configuration

1. Once you are in-game or on the track, open your right-side app bar.
2. Click on the **IsuChatAlert** icon. A quick intro animation will play, showing you where the box is. Drag it to your preferred location.
3. Open the **IsuChat Config** app from the right menu to add your keywords and tweak the visuals.
4. Click **SAVE SETTINGS**.

*💡 Tip: You can test the mention system easily by typing your own name into the in-game chat!*
